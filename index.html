Rob & Anna
Saturday, February 21st 2015

our journey

With jobs and hobbies in front of a computer screen, it comes as little surprise that Anna and Robby met online. After exchanging just 1-2 emails, the two went on a first date at a Rocky Mountain Flatbread on Main Street.

More dates of hiking, snowshoeing, skiing, biking, and camping ensued and thus began an ever-growing relationship. Robby and Anna alike enjoyed exploring the great outdoors--conquering the Lions in Squamish, Mt. Roraima in Venezeula, and Diamond Head Crater in Oahu; wisping through Grouse, Seymour, and Cypress; fording Trout Creek and Sooke Potholes; and trudging across the Juan de Fuca.

Most importantly, Robby and Anna also share mutual faith and grounding in Jesus Christ. Seeking to serve all the more effectively through complementing gifts, Robby and Anna are excited to celebrate their upcoming marriage.

our engagement

Completing the Victoria Half Marathon together on Thanksgiving weekend was a journey of perseverance and mutual encouragement. Crossing the finish line hand in hand was not only stupidly adorable, but a moment of unity and victory.

The engagement ring Robby had commissioned was complete the following Thursday. Robby then planned a trail run around Thetis Lake, scoping out the perfect proposal spot the day before. On a brisk Saturday morning, Anna and Robby started out their trail run. As he often did, Robby darted off through some trees and beckoned Anna to see the lake from a lookout. On October 18th 2014, Robby and Anna got engaged!

Upon arriving home, Anna lifted her entire bed frame to retrieve an engagement gift for Robby, a dress watch. And together they celebrated their engagement with a ring for her and a watch for him.
